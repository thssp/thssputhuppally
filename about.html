About

The Technical Higher Secondary School, Puthuppally was established in the year 1992 as the third THSS in Kerala
under the Institute of Human Resources Development, Thiruvananthapuram. The institute provides education from standard VIII to XII.
In addition to this it runs short term courses like Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Application(PGDCA), Diploma in Computer Application(DCA),
Diploma in Data Entry and Office Automation (DDET&OA).

Situated about 150mts, From Puthuppally junction and about 8kms from Kottayam. The school has an excellent atmosphere for healthy education. It provides an education which strives for excellence in pursuit of knowledge and encourages creativity and independent thinking through technical courses. It imparts an all round education giving particular emphasis on intellectual, moral and physical development. We offer grand infrastructure facilities and also aim at providing higher education standards.
